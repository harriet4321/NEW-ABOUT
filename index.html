Introduction

She Code Africa Chuka (sca_chuka) is a chapter of She Code Africa focused to empowering and celebrating ladies in tech in Chuka university.

Goals and Mission

Sca_chuka began last year in September and currently has 120 members. The main goal of Sca_chuka is to empower ladies taking STEM courses to actively develop innovations and champion them alongside their male counterparts. We believe that by doing this we can even change the African woman narrative by nurturing the ladies to occupy leadership position in STEM.
